What is the Rcik and Morty Weapons and Tools Website About

The Rick and Morty Weapons and Tools website is a webpage that allows the fans of the show "Rick and Morty" to view some interesting dangerous weapons invented by Rick that they might have missed in the show. The webpage also allows you to view some battles form Rick and Morty on Youtube where some weapons are featured. Lastly fans can go down below and vote what weapon they think does the most damage. The Rick and Morty Weapons and Tools webpage focus' more on the most popular inventions on the show, specifically ones that Rick himself has made in his garage.
